Question: Are there potentially hazardous brownfield sites in urban areas in North Carolina? If so, where are they located?

The map below shows the EPA registered brownfield sites that are located within a 1/2 mile of urban areas in North Carolina. The "Urban Areas" of NC were defined by the Department of Transportation. To create this map I used the intersect geoprocessing tool to first isolate only the urban areas in NC because the shapefile originally spanned partially across South Carolina. Then I ued the intersect tool with the browfield buffer layer and the urban areas layer to show only brownfields within that designated area. I also created the aforementioned 1/2 mile buffer using the buffer tool with the brownfield layer.
